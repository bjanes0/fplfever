Raul Jiminez Preseason Review

July 31, 2019

Raul Jiminez has been priced at 7.5 this season

After a robust season 2018/19 season, Raul Jiminez was one of the top picks for many fantasy managers due to his low price and explosive FPL returns. After scoring nearly 200 points last year, with 181, he is undoubtably on everyone's radar once again.

Preseason Review

Jiminez had a preseason filled with action for his country and has a few upcoming matches with Wolves in preseason. He played with Mexico in the CONCACAF gold cup and was able to find the net 4 times in 7 matches. Upcoming, he has 3 more matches with Wolves as they attempt to qualify for the Europa League. If he continues his form from the gold cup, he could manage to get off to a hot start this season. Overall, his preseason form has been excellent.

Initial Fixtures

At the start of the season, Wolves will face strong opposition with matches against Leicester, Manchester United, and Chelsea in their first 5. Despite this, Wolves have proven their capability to take down big teams in the past, which means Jiminez could still find points early on. Jiminez also has the bonus of rotating penalties with teammate Ruben Neves, and with the next one being Raul’s responsibility, he could help managers get off the ground in the new season.

Overall Verdict

All things considered, Jiminez should be set for another season as a great fantasy option. His 7.5 price tag isn’t too demanding for most managers and provides good value for the quality he offers.
